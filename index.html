CHANGE

Change starts with you

ElleHacks is the largest hackathon created for women and non-binary individuals of all experience levels, it is an event lead entirely by students with a background in STEM fields, proposed to challenge participants from different technical and creative disciplines to collaborate, learn, compete and show off their skills to solve real life problems in an inclusive and safe space.

Show your ambition, proudly

Any skillset is welcome at ElleHacks, and at the end of the hackathon we expect all hackers to leave with a sense of pride in their ability to solve problems. In order to do this, a community of mentors and sponsors will be available to provide tools and experience.

Frequently asked questions

Why do we need an all-female hackathon?

Women are underrepresented in the tech world, but it’s up to us women to change that! We are creating a welcoming environment to show that there is a place in tech for every lady out there while building cool projects and sharing stories at ElleHacks.

Do I need any experience in computer science to participate?

Students of all skill levels are encouraged to attend, even if you’ve never written a line of code. We’re prepared to teach you everything you need to learn right here while also supporting the most experienced hackers.

How does a team work?

Teams consist of 4 members. You can always use our facebook group and slack channel to find other individuals who might be your potential team members.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes. We are providing buses to Toronto from Montreal, Ottawa, Waterloo, Hamilton and Mississauga. Travel reimbursements will also be provided. Don't hesitate to contact us should you require travel reimbursement.

Will food and accommodation be provided?

We will be serving breakfast, lunch and dinner, and lots of snacks (all free)! We will have designated area for sleeping purposes, which students will bring their own sleeping bags to sleep. For hygienic purposes, we have included gifts in your tote bag and we also have a shower available if needed! (bring your own toothbrush!)

What should I bring?

You must bring a valid student or government issued ID card for admission. Bring your laptop, charging cables, and a sleeping bag/pillow if that makes you more comfortable for the night. We also recommend bringing toiletries and a change of clothes. Everything else will be provided!
